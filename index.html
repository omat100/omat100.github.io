A bit about me

I'm a first year Computer Engineering student. Graphics programming pulled me in early — something about understanding exactly how pixels get on screen felt worth obsessing over. When I'm not writing C++, I'm building data tools and desktop apps in Python. Currently learning OpenGL from scratch.

My interest sits at the intersection of low-level systems and visual output — I care about how pixels get on screen, how data becomes insight, and how a game engine actually works under the hood.
